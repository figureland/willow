|**Level**|**What it represents**|**Example**|**Why it matters**|
|Organisation|The account, business, project, or programme managing one or more farms|Individual grower; corporate supply-chain programme; aggregator; adviser-managed portfolio|Reporting basis and data ownership|
|Farm|A farm or operational unit|North Hill Farms|Main baseline for onboarding, insights, and reporting|
|Enterprise|The farming system or production category|Arable, permanent grassland, livestock, AD plant, horticulture, transport|Determines which onboarding requirements and modules are relevant|
|Field (land parcel)|The smallest mapped land unit Sandy needs to analyse (Land-based enterprises)|North Field, Orchard Block 3|Essential visual and analytical anchor for all spatial outputs – just relevant to Land-based enterprises|
|Season / reporting year|The time period for records, analysis, and comparison|Harvest Year 2025|Enables annual reporting, progress tracking, and year-two updates|
|Land use / cropping|What is grown or how the field is used during a given period|Winter wheat, apples, permanent grassland, conservation area|Connects land to management activities and analytical outputs|
|Operations, inputs, and observations|What happened on the field or crop cycle|Fertiliser application, drilling, spraying, soil test, yield record, fuel|Drives deeper validation, calculations, and recommendations|
